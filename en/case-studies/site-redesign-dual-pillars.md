## Background

The original KomuraSoft LLC site read consistently as a Windows contract-development site from the homepage through the company page, profile, case studies, and contact page. To add web-side inquiries naturally, the site needed to be repositioned as website development and SEO for technical B2B businesses rather than as a generic agency.

## What changed

1. The main phrase was unified around Website Development, SEO, and Windows Development.
2. The homepage was rebuilt around two entry points.
3. Navigation was reorganized into Website Development, SEO, Windows Development, Case Studies, Blog, Company, and Contact.
4. New parent pages were added for website development, SEO, and Windows development.
5. The contact page was expanded into a shared entry point.

## Why this mattered

- the site needed to stop looking Windows-only
- the Windows-side strength still had to remain visible
- the web-side offer needed to stay narrowly framed around technical and B2B businesses
