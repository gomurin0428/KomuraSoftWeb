## What you can contact us about

- Windows software contract development
- changes and maintenance for existing systems
- technical consultation, design review, and modernization planning
- investigation of hard-to-reproduce defects and long-run failures
- handling older assets involving COM / ActiveX / 32-bit / 64-bit boundaries

## Helpful information to include

- what is currently happening
- when it started
- whether there are known reproduction conditions
- what constraints or environment details matter
- what is already known so far

## What happens after you contact us

1. We review the message and, as a rule, send an acknowledgement by the next business day.
2. If needed, we ask for additional information or a short hearing.
3. We then guide you to the next step, such as technical consultation, an initial investigation, or an estimate for development or modification.
