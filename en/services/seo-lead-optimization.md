## What this service improves

- articles exist, but they do not feed into service pages
- the site is only found through brand searches
- service pages are weak even when the right query reaches them
- Google Ads is running, but the traffic does not turn into inquiries
- inquiry buttons exist, but the next step still feels unclear

## What we handle

- service-page revisions
- title, heading, and body restructuring
- internal links from articles to services
- Google Ads management with a search-focused scope
- search terms, ad copy, and landing pages
- contact-page and form improvement
- review of the key metrics to watch in Search Console, GA4, and Google Ads

## What we do not do

- ranking guarantees
- link buying
- thin mass-produced articles
- stuffing keywords that do not match reality
- ad-only outsourcing without fixing the page foundation

## How SEO and Google Ads work together

If you want more inquiries this month, start with Google Ads. If you want long-term assets, start with SEO. If you want both high-intent and comparison traffic, run them in parallel.
